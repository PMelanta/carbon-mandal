Roshan Rai

Roshan is driven by his passion for his family, friends, work, and the world. Studious and hardworking, he holds a B.E. degree in mechanical engineering.

He then went on to intern for Volvo Group and subsequently work for Forbes Marshall in the company's pilot sustainability projects. Having observed that back home, sustainable solutions and services are obscure, he founded Carbon Mandal.

With the vision clear in front of him, Roshan set out to build a firm using his first-hand experience working as a sustainability consultant and his genuine desire to bring about a transformation in the way companies run and succeed.

When he is not busy working, you can find Roshan reading non-fiction and practising yoga and grooming his stunning beard.

Bhushan Rai

Bhushan has an innate sense of dedication. He leads by example and motivates people around him to do the same. Born and raised in the beautiful Indian countryside and now living in the concrete jungles, he realizes the utmost need for sustainability management.

Bhushan holds a B.E in Mechanical Engineering and has over 12 years of experience working in the Safety & Operations Management sector in the Oil & Gas industry

An extrovert with a friendly disposition, he brings the steadiness, practicality, and financial expertise needed for the firm. He is the personification of the ideal person to look up to and provides constant support and guidance to the team.

On his off days you can find Bhushan either on the sports grounds or sipping a hot cuppa and seeking new adventures.

Siddharth Shetty

Siddharth's interest in sustainability stems from his passion for energy. Aware that clean energy is one of the driving forces towards a sustainable future, he expertises in creating an eco-friendly energy ecosystem.

Blessed with an academic mind and fuelled by his interest in the global energy transition, Siddharth first obtained his B.E in Mechanical Engineering.

He then went on to receive a scholarship for Dual Masters in the field of Energy Engineering from KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden and in Electrical Engineering for Smart Grids and Buildings from Grenoble INP ENSE3, France. His Master Thesis was on "Energy System Modelling - H2020 EMB3RS."

A national-level tennis player, Siddharth is a man of few words, and you can often find him immersed in reading. The acclaimed book 'The Shadow of the Wind' happens to his favourite book.
